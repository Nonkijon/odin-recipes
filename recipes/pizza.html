Pizza

The classic Italian dish. A margherita pizza. Unrivaled in its simplicity and taste

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Tomato
  • Dough
  • Basil
  • Oregano

Steps

  1. Roll the dough
  2. Spread the tomoato
  3. Spread the cheese
  4. Sprinkle the basil and oregano evenly over the pizza
  5. Place in the over for 20 minutes
